About Us

Quality Service & Efficiency at a Reasonable Price

Hub City Drywall is your go-to drywall contractor serving the Greater Moncton and Dieppe area. We specialize in professional drywall installation, taping, and finishing for both residential and commercial projects.

From new construction to renovations, our crew brings the expertise and work ethic to deliver clean, seamless walls — on time and on budget. No job is too big or too small.
